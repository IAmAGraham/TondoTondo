Welcome

The Space

Tondo tondo is an Italian idiom meaning "just right" — used when a wine is perfectly balanced: smooth, full, and harmonious across every note. That's the feeling we set out to create here in the North Georgia mountains.

Not too much. Not too little. A modern retreat with warm wood interiors, linen textures, and the quiet glow of firelight — designed to help you slow down, reconnect, and breathe deeply. Whether you're curled up with a novel, roasting s'mores under the stars, or exploring downtown Blue Ridge, Tondo Tondo invites you to arrive fully and leave restored.

Sleeping

Two comfortable bedrooms sleep up to 4 guests. Crisp linens, quiet surroundings, and the sounds of the North Georgia woods make for genuinely restful sleep — the kind that doesn't require a blackout mask.

Kitchen & Dining

The fully equipped kitchen has everything you need — refrigerator, oven, stovetop, microwave, coffee maker, and complete cookware. Whether you're making a slow Saturday breakfast or cooking in after a day at Mercier Orchards, the kitchen is ready for you.

Outdoor Spaces

A private hot tub for stargazing. A fire pit for evening rituals. A screened porch for morning coffee and birdsong. A BBQ grill for weekend cookouts. The outdoors at Tondo Tondo isn't an afterthought — it's the main event.

Connectivity

High-speed WiFi throughout the cabin, smart TV with streaming, and a washer and dryer for longer stays. Whether you're logging off completely or keeping one foot in the office, Tondo Tondo supports both modes.

Who Stays Here

Made For You

Couples Retreat

Soak in the hot tub beneath North Georgia stars. Wake slowly to birdsong and coffee on the porch. Wander downtown Blue Ridge for dinner and a bottle of local wine. Tondo Tondo is designed for the unhurried pace that makes couples feel like themselves again.

Remote Work Escape

Reliable high-speed WiFi, a quiet environment, and a screened porch built for thinking. If you need to log some focused hours between hikes and hot tub sessions, Tondo Tondo lets you work well — and rest even better.

Families & Friends

Two bedrooms, a full kitchen, a fire pit, and a BBQ grill. Room to spread out, cook real meals, and spend evenings together the old-fashioned way — around a fire, no agenda required.

Pet Owners

Your dog is a full guest here. The wooded setting, generous outdoor space, and genuinely pet-welcoming policy mean everyone in the family gets to unwind — fur and all.

What's Nearby

You're in the heart of North Georgia's mountain country. Here's what's waiting just outside your door.

~5 min
Downtown Blue Ridge

Restaurants, boutiques, craft breweries, and the scenic Blue Ridge Railroad all within a short drive north.

~3 mi
Mercier Orchards

Georgia's largest family orchard — pick-your-own apples, hard cider, peach wine, and farm-fresh produce year-round.

~15 min
Fall Branch Falls

A beautiful, easy waterfall hike perfect for families, dogs, and anyone who needs a forest reset.

~20 min
Ellijay

The self-proclaimed Apple Capital of Georgia — local wineries, orchards, and the unhurried energy of mountain living done right.

~25 min
Lake Blue Ridge

A pristine reservoir for kayaking, fishing, and lakeside picnics surrounded by rolling mountain views.

~30 min
Benton MacKaye Trail

Access the scenic long-distance trail winding through the Chattahoochee National Forest — solitude and scenery in equal measure.

Common Questions

FAQ

Is Tondo Tondo pet-friendly?

Yes — well-behaved pets are warmly welcome. The wooded property and outdoor spaces make it a natural fit for four-legged guests. A pet fee may apply; check the Airbnb or VRBO listing for current details.

How far is the cabin from downtown Blue Ridge?

About 5 minutes. Close enough for easy dinner reservations and a morning pastry run; far enough to feel like you've truly left it all behind.

Does the cabin have a hot tub?

Yes. A private hot tub is included and available throughout your stay — day or night, in every season.

Is there WiFi at the cabin?

Yes. High-speed WiFi is available throughout the property — reliable enough for video calls and remote work, peaceful enough that you might forget it's there.

How many people does the cabin sleep?

Tondo Tondo sleeps up to 4 guests across 2 bedrooms — ideal for couples, small families, or close friends traveling together.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies are managed through Airbnb and VRBO. Please review the policy on your chosen platform before completing your reservation, as it may vary by season.

Is there a fire pit at the cabin?

Yes. The outdoor fire pit is one of the cabin's most loved features. Wood is provided. S'mores are highly encouraged.

Does the cabin have a full kitchen?

Yes. The kitchen is fully stocked with a refrigerator, stovetop, oven, microwave, coffee maker, cookware, dishes, and everything you need to cook real meals during your stay.

Is there outdoor space for pets or kids?

Yes. The screened porch, fire pit area, BBQ patio, and surrounding wooded property all offer wonderful space for kids and pets to enjoy the outdoors.

Where exactly is Tondo Tondo located?

The cabin is located in the Cherry Log / Blue Ridge area of Gilmer County, Georgia — in the North Georgia mountains, near Blue Ridge, Ellijay, and the Chattahoochee National Forest.
